Adam Raezler

Vice President

Adam is all about victory. He likes to win and his entire career, from public policy to legislative work to campaigns, has been focused on identifying what a win looks like and how to obtain it. Adam is a big picture guy who sweats the details; he never loses sight of the primary objective, and he has the track record to back it up.

When he's not working the political universe, Adam is a passionate traveler, bike rider, and admirer of fine scotch. You will find him enjoying time with his family and Basenji puppy and he always bleeds Green and White for Michigan State University.
