Ryan Steusloff

Partner

Ryan Steusloff is one of Ragnar's most versatile assets. Ryan's advice is informed by over a decade of experience as a pollster, campaign manager, and a communications director, as well as a die hard desire to see his clients win. This is why Ragnar analysts can often be heard saying "Steusloff called me" before they launch into complicated all night analytics that often result in unique and critical insights. Not surprisingly, this has resulted in Ryan serving as the lead pollster for the Larry Hogan for Governor campaign, which has now elected the first two term Republican Governor in Maryland in over 45 years.

An avid skier and mountain biker, Ryan will brook no argument about the superiority of Michigan football. If he's not promoting the benefits of Delta and their service, he's probably throwing a ball for some lucky dog somewhere.
