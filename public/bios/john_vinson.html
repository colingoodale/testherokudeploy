John Vinson

Vice President

John is a Vice President and a 5th generation Floridian. However, given his portfolio, he should seriously consider taking up cross country skiing. His clients have already taken him from the Olympic Peninsula to the Florida Keys, and a whole lot of places in between. He’s worked for national committees and Presidential campaigns, state parties, and plenty more.

When he’s not working to find ways to win tough races you can find John boating on the Gulf of Mexico, hiking, or on his Mountain Bike.
